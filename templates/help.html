    Add your own wine

    Make sure you are in the home screen, then click the sign top right.

    Add your own wine type

    Make sure you are in the home screen, then click the icon. This will navigate to the Wine Type screen. Click the sign top right to add new Wine Type.

    Create a review

    You can review your own, or other user wines. Make sure you are in the home screen, then select a wine type from the list. This will navigate to a wine brand list. Select the brand from the list. This will navigate to the specific brand of wine you want to review. Add your review, or, read other reviews.

    View your profile

    Click your social media icon top right, and from the drop down list select "Profile". You can edit/save your nickname on this screen.

    Your wines

    Click your social media icon top right, and from the drop down list select "My Wines". This will list all your wines. Select a wine from the list, which will let you view the specific wine. Clicking on the sign top right will allow you to edit that specific wine. When editing a wine, the sign will be available top right to delete the wine if you so wish. Note that when a wine is deleted, all reviews are deleted.

    Your reviews

    Click your social media icon top right, and from the drop down list select "My Reviews". This will list all your reviews. Clicking on the sign will allow you to edit that specific review. When editing a review, the sign will be available top right to delete the review if you so wish.
